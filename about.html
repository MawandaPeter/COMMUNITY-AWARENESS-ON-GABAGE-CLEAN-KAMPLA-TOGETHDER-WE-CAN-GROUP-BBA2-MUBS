🌍 Our Story & Mission

Who We Are

EcoCycle was founded in 2022 by a group of environmental enthusiasts who saw the growing waste crisis in our communities. We started with small neighborhood recycling drives and today we have grown into a movement with over 5,000 active volunteers.

Our goal is simple: educate, empower, and execute sustainable waste management solutions. We partner with local schools, businesses, and government to create a circular economy where nothing goes to waste.

🎯 Our Vision for 2030

We envision a future where every household, school, and business has access to proper recycling facilities. EcoCycle aims to establish 10 regional recycling hubs and reduce plastic pollution by 60% in our partner cities. Together, we can create a waste-free world.
